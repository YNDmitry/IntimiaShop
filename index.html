Intimia® Breast Pillow

Have you noticed wrinkles forming on your décolleté or cleavage area? The Intimia® Pillow Bra was created to smooth out the chest while sleeping to help correct and prevent these décolletage creases. No other anti aging products work like Intimia®.

When you sleep on your side, the breasts press together causing the skin in between to pucker. What the breast pillow does is allow the breasts to remain separated while you sleep, regardless of the position. The pillow gives a soft cushioning and support to the breasts, so it feels natural and comfortable. Continuous use of the breast pillow at night effectively smoothens the chest area while adding support and comfort to the breasts. While Intimia was developed to reduce and prevent chest wrinkles, it may also reduce soreness in the breasts due to pregnancy, PMS, or breast-related surgeries.

This reduction of tenderness allowed women to sleep better at night. The pillow doesn’t require any adhesives or glue because it uses gentle yet secure soft adjustable straps that won’t cut off your blood circulation or tug on your skin. The pillow designed to fit the contours of your body will sooth and support your breasts for a great night’s sleep!
