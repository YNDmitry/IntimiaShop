Peptide Anti Wrinkle Info

Peptides can be great anti-aging ingredients for skin care products.

For specialized peptides to exert a benefit beyond that of a water-binding agent, three criteria must be met:

The peptides must be stable in their base formula.
They must be paired with a carrier that enhances their absorption into skin.
They must be able to reach their target cell groups without breaking down.
